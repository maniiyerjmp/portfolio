About Me

As a CFA Level I candidate with over five years of hands-on stock market experience, I've developed a disciplined approach to equity research and investment analysis. My journey began during my B.Com (Accounts Hons) studies at The Bhawanipur Education Society College, where I discovered my passion for financial markets and value investing principles.

I actively manage my personal portfolio, applying a blend of fundamental analysis and technical insights to make informed investment decisions.
